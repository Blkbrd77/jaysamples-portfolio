About Me

"Delivering exceptional improvements through product, service, and organizational transformations"

I'm an accomplished Program Manager with over 20 years of experience driving continuous process improvement through the strategic application of Agile frameworks, Lean processes, and modern leadership methodologies. My career spans defense, government, retail, and manufacturing industries, where I've consistently delivered high-impact results by aligning IT services with organizational goals.
